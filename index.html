Privacy Policy for C' Buddy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

C' Buddy collects the following information:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Health data (height, weight, date of birth, gender)
  • Activity level and fitness goals
  • Daily food and water intake logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized calorie and nutrition tracking
  • Calculate health metrics (BMI, BMR, TDEE)
  • Show your progress over time
  • Send daily tracking reminders (if enabled)

3. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Supabase — secure database storage for your account and health data
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements to non-premium users. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's encrypted database. We do not sell your personal information to any third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

C' Buddy is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
